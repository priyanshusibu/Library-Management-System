About Us :

Welcome to Central Library , a place where knowledge meets community. Our mission is to provide access to a wealth of information and resources for all.

Our friendly staff assists with research, recommendations, and reference services. Join us for author talks, workshops, and reading challenges. At Central Library,Bhubaneswar, we believe in equal access to information, creating a welcoming space for everyone.
